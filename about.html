About Me

// Who I am

I'm Mathew Duclos, a cinematographer and creative director based in Austin. I make video for social media, small business content, and short form production, and I take on bigger short film or brand production work when the project calls for it.

// What I'm building toward

Most of what I make is meant to be felt before it's explained. The goal isn't narration doing the work, it's a shot, a cut, a rhythm that sits with someone after they've looked away. That lingering is the actual value: people remember what made them feel something, and that memory becomes attention for whatever you're building.

// Get in touch

Currently taking on new work in Austin and the surrounding area. If you've got something that needs a real point of view behind the camera, let's talk.
